Get Involved → Our Mission

Housing is a foundation,
not a privilege.

BizBridge is a Tennessee-registered nonprofit that develops, rehabilitates, and manages affordable housing — connecting underserved families to the stability they deserve through Section 8 programs and federal partnerships.

What We Stand For

Built on three principles.

01

Dignity by Design

Every unit we develop is held to quality standards that go beyond minimum compliance. People deserve more than the bare minimum.

02

Financially Sustainable

Federal funding used strategically so every project we build remains viable and serves the community for decades to come.

03

Community Rooted

We work alongside residents, local PHAs, and municipalities — not around them. Real change starts with real relationships.

What We Do

Built to deliver.

01

New Construction

Ground-up development of affordable rental units built to HUD quality standards and long-term community needs.

02

Property Rehabilitation

Acquiring and transforming distressed, blighted properties into stable, dignified affordable homes.

03

Section 8 Leasing

Housing Choice Voucher and project-based Section 8 management with end-to-end PHA coordination and compliance.

04

Federal Grant Access

Navigating HOME, CDBG, LIHTC, and THDA programs to unlock non-dilutive capital for every project.

05

Community Partnerships

Collaborating with PHAs, municipalities, and social services organizations for wraparound resident support.

06

Property Management

Ongoing compliance, maintenance coordination, and tenant services ensuring long-term housing quality.

How It Works

From site to lease-up.

Step 01

Site Identification

Sourcing through HUD HomeStore, county tax records, and community referrals for viable acquisition targets.

Step 02

Funding & Finance

Securing capital through THDA, HOME, CDBG, and LIHTC with full compliance documentation.

Step 03

Development

Managing construction or rehab to HUD standards with licensed contractors and regular inspections.

Step 04

Lease-Up & Manage

PHA Section 8 certification, resident placement, and long-term ongoing compliance management.
