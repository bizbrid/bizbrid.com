What We Do
Built to deliver.
01

New Construction

Ground-up development of affordable rental units built to HUD quality standards and long-term community needs.

02

Property Rehabilitation

Acquiring and transforming distressed, blighted properties into stable, dignified affordable homes.

03

Section 8 Leasing

Housing Choice Voucher and project-based Section 8 management with end-to-end PHA coordination.

04

Federal Grant Access

Navigating HOME, CDBG, LIHTC, and THDA programs to unlock non-dilutive capital for every project.

05

Community Partnerships

Working alongside PHAs, municipalities, and social service organizations for wraparound resident support.

06

Property Management

Ongoing compliance, maintenance coordination, and tenant services ensuring long-term housing quality.

Our Mission

Housing is a foundation,
not a privilege.

BizBridge exists to close the gap between underserved communities and the stable, quality housing they deserve — using every tool available, from federal programs to local partnerships.

01

Dignity by Design

Every unit held to quality standards that go beyond minimum compliance.

02

Financially Sustainable

Federal funding used strategically so every project remains viable for decades.

03

Community Rooted

We work alongside residents and local government — not around them.

How It Works
From site to lease-up.
Step 01

Site Identification

Sourcing through HUD HomeStore, county tax records, and community referrals for viable properties.

Step 02

Funding & Finance

Securing capital through THDA, HOME, CDBG, and LIHTC with full compliance documentation.

Step 03

Development

Managing construction or rehab to HUD standards with licensed contractors and ongoing inspections.

Step 04

Lease-Up & Manage

PHA Section 8 certification, resident placement, and long-term compliance management.
