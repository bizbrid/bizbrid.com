Get Involved → Our Mission

Housing is a foundation,
not a privilege.

BizBridge is a Tennessee-registered nonprofit developing, rehabilitating, and managing affordable housing — connecting underserved families to the stability they deserve through Section 8 and federal housing programs.

What We Stand For

Three core principles.

Dignity by Design

Every unit we develop is held to quality standards that go beyond minimum compliance. People deserve more than the bare minimum.

Financially Sustainable

Federal funding used strategically so every project remains viable and serves the community for decades to come.

Community Rooted

We work alongside residents, local PHAs, and municipalities — not around them. Real change starts with real relationships.

What We Do

Built to deliver.

New Construction

Ground-up development of affordable rental units built to HUD quality standards.

Property Rehabilitation

Transforming distressed, blighted properties into dignified affordable homes.

Section 8 Leasing

HCV and project-based Section 8 management with full PHA coordination.

Federal Grant Access

Navigating HOME, CDBG, LIHTC, and THDA programs to fund every project.

Community Partnerships

Working alongside PHAs, municipalities, and social service organizations.

Property Management

Ongoing compliance, maintenance, and tenant services for long-term quality.

How It Works

From site to lease-up.

Site Identification

Sourcing through HUD HomeStore, county tax records, and community referrals for viable properties.

Funding & Finance

Securing capital through THDA, HOME, CDBG, and LIHTC with full compliance documentation.

Development

Managing construction or rehab to HUD standards with licensed contractors and inspections.

Lease-Up & Manage

PHA Section 8 certification, resident placement, and long-term compliance management.
